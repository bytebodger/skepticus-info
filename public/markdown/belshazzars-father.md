**Belshazzar’s Lineage (Daniel 5)**
In the Book of Daniel, Belshazzar is described as the King of Babylon and the "son" of Nebuchadnezzar.

**The Error:** Historically, the last king of Babylon was **Nabonidus**, not Belshazzar. Belshazzar was Nabonidus' son and served as co-regent, but he wasn't related to Nebuchadnezzar.

**The Succession:** There were several kings between Nebuchadnezzar and Nabonidus (Evil-Merodach, Neriglissar, etc.), making the "son" claim factually incorrect in a genealogical sense.
