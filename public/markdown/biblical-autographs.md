We don't have any. The exact number is **zero**.

Not a single original autograph—the actual document penned by the author or their immediate scribe—survives for any book of either the Old or New Testament.

Every biblical text in existence today is a copy of a copy. The original documents were written on highly perishable materials like papyrus and parchment, which easily deteriorated from heavy use, moisture, and time. By the time textual preservation became a systematic priority centuries later, the originals had long since vanished.
