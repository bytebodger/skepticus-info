The Conquest of Jericho and Ai (Joshua 6–8)
The Book of Joshua describes a military "blitzkrieg" where Israelites destroyed fortified Canaanite cities around 1400–1200 BCE.

**The Error:** Archaeological excavations at Jericho show that the city’s massive walls had already fallen or the site was largely uninhabited during the Late Bronze Age (the time of Joshua).

**The Irony of Ai:** The city "Ai" literally means **"The Ruin"** in Hebrew. Archaeology confirms it was a heap of rubble for over 1,000 years before the Israelites arrived. It wasn't a fortified city for Joshua to conquer.
